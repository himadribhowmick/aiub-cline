AIUB Admission Requirements

1. Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm)

  • Applicants must be permanent residents of Bangladesh.
  • Foreign applicants may apply only for reserved seats allocated by the Government.
  • Minimum total GPA of 8.00 in SSC & HSC, with minimum GPA 3.50 in each.
  • HSC: GPA 3.50 in Chemistry and Biology; GPA 3.00 in Physics and Mathematics.
  • A-Level: Grade 'B' in Chemistry and Biology; Grade 'C' in Physics and Math.
  • Math-deficient students must take an extra 3-credit Math course.
  • Valid for current year or past 2 years only.
  • Foreign applicants need an equivalence certificate from the Pharmacy Council.

2. EEE / IPE / COE / Architecture / B.Sc. (CSE)

  • Total GPA of 5.00, with minimum 2.50 in Science background. Math is required in HSC.
  • O-Level GPA 2.5 (no grade below D), A-Level GPA 2.0 (Math must be included).
  • Only 1 'E' grade allowed in combined O & A Levels (except Math & Physics).
  • IB/High School Diploma requires minimum grade B in Math.
  • GED results are not accepted.

3. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

  • Total GPA of 5.00, minimum GPA 2.50 required.
  • O-Level GPA 2.5 (no grade below D), A-Level GPA 2.0 in 2 subjects.
  • 1 'E' grade is acceptable (excluding key subjects).
  • IB/High School Diploma requires minimum grade B.
  • GED results are not accepted.

4. Arts & Social Sciences (BA English, MMC, BSS Economics, LLB)

  • Total GPA of 5.00, minimum GPA 2.50 required.
  • O-Level GPA 2.5, A-Level GPA 2.0 in 2 subjects.
  • 1 'E' grade is acceptable (excluding key subjects).
  • IB/High School Diploma requires minimum grade C.
  • GED results are not accepted.

Required Documents

  • Attested photocopy of all mark sheets, certificates/testimonials.
  • 3 recent formal passport-sized photographs.
  • A-Level students: A2 Statement of Entry & Mark Sheets.
  • HSC students: Appeared Certificate, Admit Card, and Registration Card.
  • Freedom Fighter applicants: multiple documents including National ID, Mukti Barta, and Gazette.
  • FF quota candidates must present originals during admission.

Written Exam Waiver Conditions

  • SAT score of ≥ 1650 (out of 2400)
  • SAT score of ≥ 1106 (out of 1600)
  • Applicants will be called for a viva interview.
