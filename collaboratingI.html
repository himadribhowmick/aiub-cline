Collaborating Institutes

Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh (SRBD)

Type: Industry Collaboration

Focus: Mobile Application Development and Research on Tizen OS

Details: SRBD, a subsidiary of Samsung Electronics, collaborates with AIUB to integrate Tizen platform development into the curriculum, enhancing skills in wearable and IoT technologies.

BRAC IT Services Ltd. (biTS)

Type: Industry Collaboration

Focus: IT Solutions and Services

Details: biTS partners with AIUB to provide end-to-end IT solutions across various industries, aiming to enhance operational efficiency through technology.

Department of Linguistics, University of Dhaka

Type: Academic Collaboration

Focus: Linguistics Research and Development

Details: Collaboration aims to foster research and academic exchange in the field of linguistics.

BJIT Academy Ltd.

Type: Industry Collaboration

Focus: IT Skill Development

Details: Partnership focuses on addressing skill gaps and producing industry-ready IT professionals through specialized training programs.

University of Trento

Type: Academic Mobility Program

Focus: Student Exchange for MSCS, MEEE, and MTEL Programs

Details: Mobility program allows eligible AIUB students to take courses at the University of Trento, promoting international academic exposure.
