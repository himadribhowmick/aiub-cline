Innovations

AIUB Robotic Crew (ARC)

Project: University Rover Challenge (URC)

Details: Participated in URC by designing and building a rover for early explorers on Mars, held annually at the Mars Desert Research Station.

Solar Electric Vehicle

Focus: Environmentally friendly and economically viable solar cars

Details: Developed three prototypes to reduce CO2 emissions and limit global temperature rise through collaboration between faculty and students.

Let's Furnish

Type: Furniture and appliances rental platform

Details: Bangladesh's only platform where people can rent/subscribe to furniture and appliances as long as they want. Contributors: B M Mohaiminul Islam Rahul, Efaz Ahmed, Tauhid Ul Alam.

Design and Implementation of A Human Prosthetic Hand

Focus: Affordable prosthetic solutions

Details: Aimed to provide a cheaper alternative to robotic prosthetics for individuals disabled due to accidents or wartime injuries.

Student of AIUB - Winner of ‘Joy Bangla Youth Award’ 2015

Event: Joy Bangla Youth Award

Details: Recognized for contributions in various fields during the ceremony organized by the Center for Research and Information on 2nd May 2015.

Engineering Students Awarded in Power & Energy Week Innovation Expo 2018

Event: Power & Energy Hackathon 2018

Details: Ministry of Power, Energy & Mineral Resources hosted a three-day event to inspire young ideas and dynamic innovations in the energy industry of Bangladesh.

Assistive Exoskeleton for Paralyzed People

Focus: Mobility aid for paralyzed individuals

Details: Developed a hydraulic-powered exoskeleton built on industrial steel with a synthetic hydraulic system to help paralyzed people move their legs and walk.
