Admission Requirements

To apply for undergraduate programs, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 in both SSC and HSC examinations.
  • For O-Level and A-Level students, a minimum of 5 subjects in O-Level and 2 subjects in A-Level with minimum grade 'C'.
  • Applicants must have Mathematics at HSC or A-Level.

Required Documents

  • Attested copies of all academic certificates and transcripts.
  • Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Copy of National ID or Birth Certificate.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to complete your application:

  1. Visit the AIUB Admission Portal.
  2. Register for a new account or log in to your existing account.
  3. Fill out the online application form with accurate information.
  4. Upload the required documents.
  5. Submit the application and pay the application fee.
